STATIC (@CUT2STATIC)

I joined and helped found a multimedia page called Static, where we host music and art related events, such as online music festivals (referred to as URLFests), interviews (such as this one!), and cover art contests. I play a role as an Organiser, Graphic Designer, Animator and VFX Artist, producing overlays, graphics and editing livestreams.

SEVENTHCHAPTER (@SEVENTHCHAPTER.WORLD)

This was my first attempt at a visual arts collective. I produced visualisers for artists, focusing a lot on digital distortion of videos, 3D models and images. The vision was primarily focused on an older, grungier, more pixellated artstyle, and is one that continues to influence me to this day. I left the project after about a year, as I felt a lot more confident to turn my work into a personal brand after receiving praise from my peers.

Artists Against Genocide (@artistsagainstg3nocide)

Founder and lead creator for Artists Against Genocide, a non-profit focused on providing fundraising and aid to displaced families in Gaza, Sudan and Congo. I produce videos, graphics, and manage the Instagram and Tiktok to garner attention and donations for families, as well as working in tangent with Gaza Soup Kitchen, Operation Olive Branch, and Project HOPE. We have raised roughly $20,000 as of November 2025.
